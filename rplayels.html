Understanding Chess Ratings

Elo Rating System

The Elo rating system is a method for calculating the relative skill levels of players in competitor-versus-competitor games such as chess.

  • Players gain rating points by winning games and lose points by losing games
  • The amount of points gained or lost depends on the rating difference between opponents
  • Higher rated players gain fewer points when beating lower rated players
